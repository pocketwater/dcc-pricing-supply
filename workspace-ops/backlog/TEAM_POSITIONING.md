# Operational Intelligence Team

## Team Name
Operational Intelligence (Ops Intelligence)

## Organizational Role

We own the operational data control layer:
- Ingest
- Validation
- Crosswalks
- Resolution workflows
- Deterministic publish

BI teams consume.
Operations executes.
We ensure trust.

- Under COO (Operations Control)
- Under CIO/CTO (Data Platform Control)
- Hybrid model in mid-sized organizations

We are not:

- IT ticket handlers
- Ad-hoc report writers

We are:
The system integrity layer.
